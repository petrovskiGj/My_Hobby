ABOUT ME

Hello friends , in these few lines of text I would like to say a few things about myself and my lifestyle. Hello again my name is Gorgi Petrovski and I live in Skopje, Macedona. I am a student at the Faculty of Information Sciences and Computer Engineering in Skopje. The specific direction I fell in love with in my high school days is programming, and I see it as my future job. Like everyone else, I like to fill my free time with some of my hobbies or various interesting activities that I can do alone, but sometimes I'm also in the mood with company. One of my most favorite hobbies is photography, I enjoy doing this in my spare time, sometimes I take pictures at some parties or weddings. I often take pictures of my friends as well. I am an adventurous spirit and a passionate lover of nature and hiking. I enjoy long walks in nature and the natural beauties that my country offers. Often when I hike or visit a new place I like to take pictures. Apart from nature, I also like to travel around the world.
